Big O Notation

Understanding and improving upon your code's efficiency

Computer scientists use Big O notation to describe how efficient their code is. And by efficiency, I mean runtime. In truth, it’s more complicated than that, but that’s the core concept. It’s important to us as software engineers and web developers because knowing how to measure code’s efficiency is the first step to improving it. Obviously, the less efficient the code is and the longer the runtime, the longer the delay is for the user.

It’s also pretty common for big o notation to be brought up in an interview setting. Even if you’ve written a function that works perfectly, your interviewer may ask you to make an adjustment to improve upon its runtime. Knowing how to measure, communicate about, and improve your function’s efficiency is important.
